Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 26, 2025

1. Introduction

IndGo Air Virtual ("VA", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our service. The types of information we collect can be broken down into the following categories:

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Registration Information: When you register, we collect your name (or username), email address, Infinite Flight Community (IFC) username, and a password (which is stored in a hashed format).
  • Profile Information: You may voluntarily provide additional information for your public profile, such as a biography, a link to your YouTube channel, and a custom profile picture.
  • User-Uploaded Content: We collect files you voluntarily upload, primarily verification images (e.g., flight summary screenshots) required for manual PIREP submissions.

B. Information from Third Parties

  • Discord Integration: If you choose to link your Discord account, we receive your Discord username and unique ID from Discord to verify your identity and potentially assign roles on our server.
  • SimBrief Flight Plans: When you generate a flight plan using our SimBrief integration, we retrieve detailed operational flight plan (OFP) data from SimBrief, which includes route, fuel, weight, and weather information.

C. Information Collected Automatically

  • Operational Data: We automatically collect and generate data related to your VA activities. This includes your assigned callsign, current rank, total flight hours, duty status (On Duty/On Rest), PIREP details (routes, aircraft, flight times), and your last known airport location.
  • ACARS Flight Tracking Data: For flights tracked with our ACARS system, we collect live telemetry data from your flight simulator session, including your aircraft's position (latitude, longitude, altitude), speed, and heading.
  • Usage Data: Like most web services, we may collect standard log data, which can include your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the dates/times of your access.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our VA and its services.
  • To manage your account, including authenticating you, tracking your flight hours, and managing your progression through the ranks.
  • To process and validate PIREPs, whether submitted manually or tracked via ACARS.
  • To display your profile and activity on public-facing parts of our website, such as the Pilot Roster, Staff page, and Leaderboards.
  • To communicate with you, including sending important notifications regarding your account status (e.g., promotion test requirements) or PIREP status.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and ensure the integrity and fairness of our operations.
  • To provide community features, such as linking your VA profile to your Discord identity.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Publicly on our Website: To foster a community environment, certain information is public by default. This includes your Name/Username, Callsign, Rank, and Flight Hours, which are visible on our pilot roster and leaderboards. Your email and password are never made public.
  • With Third-Party Service Providers: We use third-party services to help us operate our VA. These services are contractually obligated to protect your data. They include:
    • Hosting Providers (Render.com, MongoDB): For hosting our database and backend application.
    • File Storage (Amazon Web Services S3 or similar): For secure storage of user-uploaded images (profile pictures, PIREP verification images).
    • ACARS Service Provider: For live flight tracking and data collection during flight.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

5. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard practices, such as hashing passwords and using secure connections (HTTPS), to protect your information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, primarily for the duration your account is active. Upon account deletion, your personal data will be permanently and irreversibly removed from our systems.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data. You can update most of your profile information through the dashboard. To request a full account and data deletion, please contact a member of the VA Staff. Please note that deleting your account and data is irreversible and will result in the forfeiture of all flight hours, rank, and status within the VA.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact a member of the VA Staff Team through our official Discord server.
